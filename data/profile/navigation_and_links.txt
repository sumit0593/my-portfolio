# WEBSITE NAVIGATION, SECTIONS, AND RESOURCE LINKS GUIDE

This guide provides details on how to navigate the portfolio site, descriptions of the sections on each page, and a curated list of all relevant project, social, resume, and certification links.

---

## 1. ROUTES AND NAVIGATION OVERVIEW
The portfolio is structured across four main page routes:

1. **Dashboard Hub (`/` or `/dashboard`)**: The main interactive single-page dashboard containing all primary sections.
2. **Projects Listing Page (`/projects`)**: A dedicated interface for browsing, filtering, and studying all of Sumit's projects using interactive categories and tags.
3. **AI Tools Marketplace (`/tools`)**: A hub containing advanced AI utilities, including:
   - **Resume Analyzer (`/tools/resume-analyzer`)**: Evaluates CVs against JDs using Gemini.
   - **Stubs for other tools**: Resume Builder, JD Matcher, LinkedIn Summary Generator, Cover Letter Generator, Skill Gap Analyzer, Interview Prep, Description Optimizer, Cold Email Generator, Prompt Generator.
4. **Services Page (`/services`)**: Showcases professional freelance services (AI Chatbots, RAG Pipelines, Multi-Agent Systems, Full-Stack development).

---

## 2. DASHBOARD PAGE SECTIONS (10 SECTIONS DETAIL)
The main single-page hub is divided into **10 distinct sections** to provide a rich, modular experience:

*   **SECTION 1: Global Navigation & Header (Top Navbar)**
    - *Purpose*: Provides navigation shortcuts (Home, About, Skills, Experience, Projects, Gallery, Contact), dark mode controls, and session options (profile avatar and log out).
    - *How to navigate*: Anchored to the top of the viewport at all times.

*   **SECTION 2: Home / Hero Section (`#home`)**
    - *Purpose*: A premium, high-impact intro showing Sumit's dynamic role titles, subtitle, and primary call-to-action buttons.
    - *How to navigate*: Click "Home" in the navbar or go to `/dashboard#home`.

*   **SECTION 3: Experience Metric Section**
    - *Purpose*: Displays key metrics (e.g., 2+ Years Experience, 10+ Projects, Certifications, DSA Problem Solving counts) in a modern grid.
    - *How to navigate*: Located directly below the Hero section.

*   **SECTION 4: Resume Download & Preview Section**
    - *Purpose*: Features an interactive document viewer/preview alongside direct download links.
    - *How to navigate*: Located in the About/Profile card area.

*   **SECTION 5: Featured Projects Section (`#projects`)**
    - *Purpose*: Renders a 3D-tilt grid of featured projects (AI Loan Advisor, Scam Guard AI, HireFlow AI, and Portfolio AI) complete with tech badges, metrics, and links.
    - *How to navigate*: Click "Projects" in the navbar or go to `/dashboard#projects`.

*   **SECTION 6: Skills Scene (`#skills`)**
    - *Purpose*: Renders an interactive Three.js 3D orbiting sphere scene representing Generative AI, AI Engineering, Databases, Full Stack, and MLOps/DevOps.
    - *How to navigate*: Click "Skills" in the navbar or go to `/dashboard#skills`.

*   **SECTION 7: AI Tools Marketplace Preview**
    - *Purpose*: Showcases card pre-views of the AI tools available on `/tools` (e.g., Resume Analyzer).
    - *How to navigate*: Directly below the Skills orbit section.

*   **SECTION 8: Career Experience Timeline (`#experience`)**
    - *Purpose*: Renders a chronological journey of professional positions (Kent RO Systems, TechnoIdentity) using icons, calendars, and description lists.
    - *How to navigate*: Click "Experience" in the navbar or go to `/dashboard#experience`.

*   **SECTION 9: Gallery / Learning Section (`#gallery`)**
    - *Purpose*: An interactive tabbed interface displaying LeetCode/DSA problem-solving stats and certificates with zoom-able previews.
    - *How to navigate*: Click "Gallery" in the navbar or go to `/dashboard#gallery`.

*   **SECTION 10: Interactive Contact Section (`#contact`)**
    - *Purpose*: Renders a custom email form (with validation) and lists direct social/contact profiles.
    - *How to navigate*: Click "Contact" in the navbar or go to `/dashboard#contact`.
